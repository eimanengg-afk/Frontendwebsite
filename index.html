About EEC

Independent engineering in Pakistan, since 2001.

Eiman Engineering Corp is an independent engineering and project delivery company operating since 2001, with full lifecycle accountability from concept to commissioning.

Founded in 2001 with Saudi Aramco's Haradh Gas Field under JGC Japan and GEA BTT France, and followed in the same year by the SS 304 U-tube bundle procurement from Shangzhu Walsin Specialty Steel, China for Dynopak via DDFC Lahore — EEC has since delivered across oil & gas, petrochemical, chemical and industrial sectors, combining international standards with a regional cost base.
